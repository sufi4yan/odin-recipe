idli Fries

Description

A modern take on the popular French Fries, these idli fries are a perfect snack for any occasion, can also be served as an appetizer before any meal. Idli Fries are a great way to use up those leftover idlis. Sauteed with onion and garlic, it will give your taste buds a different flavour.

Ingredients

  • 10 Idli
  • 200 ml sunflower oil
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 5 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 2 teaspoon powdered red chilli
  • 2 pinch salt
  • 10 gm minced garlic

Steps

  1. Prepare the mixture.
  2. Add fries in it.
  3. Add fries into the oil pan.
  4. Take it out when it looks right.
  5. Enjoy!
